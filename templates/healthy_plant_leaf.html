Disease Name :

Foliar disease on the plant leaf .

Causes of Disease:

In many cases the complex of foliar disease causing organisms (Alternaria, Cercospora, Stemphyllium, etc.) that cause these diseases are typically linked to potassium deficiency in cotton leaves, but are not generally related to a soil deficiency

Solution for Disease:

The best way to treat suspected bacterial infections is to cut out all infected plant parts in order to prevent further spread. You can also use a bactericide in the early stages of the disease.
